About Me

I am a music composer and sound designer focused on creating immersive acoustic environments for video games and interactive media. My passion lies in blending grand orchestral arrangements with modern synthesizers and analog instruments, though I also delve deep into ambient electronic soundscapes and chiptune, bridging the gap between music production and gameplay by implementing adaptive audio that elevates the player's emotional journey.
