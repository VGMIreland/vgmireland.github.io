Behind the Project

Garman Blue is the solo artist project created by the composer behind VGM Ireland. While the latter focuses on adaptive scoring, sound design, and game audio implementation, Garman Blue serves as an unrestricted creative outlet for original, more personal compositions.

By pushing the boundaries of analog synthesizers, modern beats, and melodic synth-work, the music released under this moniker feeds back into the atmospheric depth and technical craftsmanship found in all VGM Ireland productions.
